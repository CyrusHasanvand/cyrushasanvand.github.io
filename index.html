About

I am a passionate researcher specializing in universal autonomous intelligent systems and online learning for nonstationary environments. My work focuses on bridging advanced mathematics and statistics with cognitive-inspired AI to solve fundamental challenges in computational and artificial intelligence.

I received the B.Sc. degree in electrical engineering from Shiraz University, Shiraz, Iran, in 2015, and the M.Sc. degree in control engineering from Tarbiat Modares University, Tehran, Iran, in 2019.

My main research interests include pattern recognition, cognitive science, memory stability and plasticity analysis, continual learning, statistical modeling, uncertainty quantification, robust decision making, and active concept drift adaptation.
