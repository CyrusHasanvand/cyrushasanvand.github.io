About

I am a passionate researcher specializing in autonomous intelligent systems and online learning for nonstationary environments. My work focuses on bridging advanced mathematics and statistics with cognitive-inspired AI to solve fundamental challenges in autonomous learning and universal machine intelligence.

Research

I work on real-time uncertainty quantification and adaptive learning. My contributions include UFAREX and REIVAX, which are intelligent platforms for concept drift adaptation and robustness in nonstationary environments.

Publications

  • UFAREX: IEEE Transactions on Systems, Man, and Cybernetics

Teaching

I have served as a teaching assistant for courses in control systems, online learning, and AI, and have led graduate workshops on fault detection and adaptive models.
